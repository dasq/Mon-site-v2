About

Graduated from classical and jazz schools, I have investigated and performed in all kind of music from world music to hip-hop and everything in between. With this solo project, I have found my path in the music genres and I offer today a travelling experience through modern fingerstyle guitar. My songs demonstrate clearly the long world travel I just accomplished recently and got my inspirations from. Orient, India or America are just a part of where you will find yourself relaxing and dreaming…

Pictures


The last world trip I have made remains to be my main inspiration for my compositions. I have been influenced by the lovely people I met either in Sri Lanka or New Zealand and by the outstanding landscapes of the desert in Dubaï and also by the beauties of the ocean in Thaïland. It is difficult to transcribe those wonderful discoveries I made and that have influenced me forever. This is why I share with you my pictures for this unique experience via my Instagram.
